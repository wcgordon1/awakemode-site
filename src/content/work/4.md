Developers use AwakeMode during long compile and benchmark cycles.

## Goals

1. Avoid sleep during long builds.
2. Keep session controls close to workflow.
3. Provide browser and extension options.

## Result

Engineers spend less time restarting interrupted runs and more time shipping code.
