AwakeMode keeps export machines active while long rendering pipelines run.

## Goals

1. Avoid sleep interruptions during multi-hour render jobs.
2. Set predictable timer windows for overnight tasks.
3. Keep status visible at a glance.

## Result

Teams reduced failed overnight exports and no longer relied on manual terminal commands.
