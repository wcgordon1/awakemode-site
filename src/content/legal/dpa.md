## Data Processing Addendum

This DPA describes how AwakeMode processes personal data on behalf of customers where applicable.

### Processing purpose

Data is processed only to provide and support the AwakeMode service.

### Security

AwakeMode applies reasonable technical and organizational controls to protect data.

### Subprocessors

We may use subprocessors for hosting, analytics, and support operations. These providers are bound by data protection obligations.

### Requests

Data processing and compliance requests can be submitted through the contact page.
