## Privacy Policy

AwakeMode collects only the information needed to operate and improve the service.

### What we collect

- Account information you provide.
- Basic usage telemetry (for reliability and performance).
- Support messages you send through contact forms.

### How we use data

- To deliver the service.
- To improve product quality and reliability.
- To respond to support and security issues.

### Data sharing

We do not sell personal data. We share data only with service providers required to run AwakeMode.

### Your choices

You can request account and data access or deletion through the contact page.
