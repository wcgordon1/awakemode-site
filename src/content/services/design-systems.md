## Shared Design System

AwakeMode keeps the website and extension visually aligned using shared tokens.

### Included

- Shared color, type, and spacing variables.
- Reusable controls for status, timers, and actions.
- Consistent behavior patterns across pages.
- Fast iteration for new product surfaces.
